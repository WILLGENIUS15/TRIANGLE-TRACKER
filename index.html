Triangle Tracker

This is an application that determines whether a set of three numbers
can either make a scalene,Equilateral and or Isosceles triangle.

DEFINITION OF A TRIANGLE

EQUILATERAL:

A triangle that has all sides equal

ISCOCELESS:

a triangle in which two sides have the same length

SCALENE:

A scalene triangle is a triangle that
has three unequal sides
